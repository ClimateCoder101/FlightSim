How to Play

Game Controls

  • W: Move Forward
  • S: Move Backward
  • A: Move Left
  • D: Move Right
  • Arrow Up: Move Up
  • Arrow Down: Move Down
  • Space: Shoot

Objective

Your goal is to score points by shooting down enemy airplanes while avoiding their bullets and staying above the terrain.

Tips

  • Use the terrain to your advantage to avoid enemy fire.
  • Keep an eye on your speed and adjust it to maintain control.
  • Plan your movements to intercept enemy airplanes effectively.
